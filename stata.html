Econometric Modeling

Regression Modeling and Interpretation in Microeconomics

In this project I will use STATA to analyze how variables like apartment size, distance from campus, size of property, and building age are factored to determine housing prices in the Ann Arbor housing market. I'll perform an OLS regression to estimate marginal effects and evalute price elasticity, identifying which housing traits most strongly drive rental and sale values.

Properties will be measured in average price per square foot.
